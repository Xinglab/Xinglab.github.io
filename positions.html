Join Us

We are recruiting students and postdoctoral fellows.

For undergraduate and graduate students, please contact yxingucla.edu. We have openings for motivated students from various scientific disciplines (biology, medicine, statistics, engineering, etc) who wish to work at the interface of genomics, bioinformatics and medicine.

For inquiries of postdoctoral fellow positions, please send a CV to yxingucla.edu. Please include a list of publications and details of three references. Qualified candidates are expected to hold a PhD in bioinformatics or related fields.
